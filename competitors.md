# ADR Network — Competitors

## Primary: Driver Hire
- **URL:** driverhire.co.uk
- **Positioning:** "UK's largest specialist logistics recruiter"
- **Offices:** 120+ (franchise model)
- **Heritage:** 40+ years
- **Certifications:** ISO 9001, REC, RHA, Investors in People, Great Place to Work
- **Published rates:** £14.50–£18/hr
- **Technology:** None (no proprietary VMS)
- **Key vulnerability:** Franchise inconsistency — quality varies by local franchisee

## Secondary: Driver Require
- **URL:** driverrequire.co.uk
- **Offices:** 10 (regional, not national)
- **Google rating:** 4.8/5 from 150+ reviews
- **Named clients:** Ocado, Zeus Packaging
- **Published rates:** Yes
- **Technology:** None
- **Key vulnerability:** Geographic ceiling — cannot serve multi-site national accounts

## Competitive Verdict
ADR holds stronger underlying credentials (tSOLV, Pertemps Group, FTSE 100 clients) but loses the perception battle because trust signals are not visible. Driver Hire wins on perceived scale; Driver Require wins on trust signal display. ADR should win on technology + consistency + enterprise client scale — but currently presents as generic.
